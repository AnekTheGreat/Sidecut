How to use SideCut

Getting music into your library
SideCut plays audio files already saved on your device — it doesn't stream or fetch anything itself. One way to get songs onto SideCut is via Spotify. Click onto your playlist, Click share, then copy the link. After that go to Spoticatch, Spotisaver or any other Spotify to mp3 downloader. after that click files and select all of your songs and import them. Now you have all of your songs locally stored right on yout device in one specific setting.
•Tap + Files to pick one or more individual songs
•Tap + Add folder to grab a whole folder at once — handy for a full album or a Downloads folder
•Use Import library to restore a full backup .zip, including playlists and tags
Playing music
•Tap any song in your list to start playing it
•Use the mini player at the bottom for play/pause, skip, and shuffle
•Tap the ⋮ (kebab) next to a song for more options — add to playlist, play next, delete, and more
Customizing the look
•Open Settings (gear icon) → Theme to pick a look, or build your own custom theme
•Under Settings → Glow, mix and match edge lines, blur bands, and corner glow — all independent toggles, all customizable
•Settings → Playback has crossfade, gapless mode, and normalization
•Settings → Equalizer has 8 adjustable bands plus presets
Playlists & organizing
•Create playlists from the Playlists tab, then add songs via the kebab menu on any track
•Long-press a song row to select multiple tracks at once
•Export library anytime to back everything up as a .zip you can restore later
Stuck on something, or a button not working the way you'd expect? Use the thumbs-down/feedback option where you're chatting with Claude to report it.
